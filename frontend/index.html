Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?

Currently CSV (.csv) and Excel (.xls, .xlsx). Support for Parquet, JSON, and more formats is planned for future versions.

How accurate are the suggestions?

Suggestions are rule-based and statistical (missing %, cardinality, column names, patterns). They work well for ~85–90% of real-world tabular data, but always verify before applying — especially for domain-specific rules.

Does it delete my data?

No. All processing happens in memory. Nothing is saved to disk or sent anywhere. When you close the tab, data is cleared.

Can I customize cleaning rules?

Yes — edit the JSON config directly or use natural language commands (e.g. "fill missing Gross with median", "sort by Date descending").

Is it secure for sensitive data?

Completely local-first. No data leaves your machine unless you choose to download/upload elsewhere. Ideal for private or proprietary datasets.

About Us

CSV Cleaning Bot is a privacy-first, local-first tool designed to help data professionals, analysts, students and businesses quickly prepare messy tabular data for analysis, visualization or machine learning — without ever uploading files to the cloud.

Built with FastAPI, Pandas, and modern browser technologies, it emphasizes:

  • Zero data collection or storage
  • Smart, explainable rule suggestions (no black-box AI in current version)
  • Full transparency: detailed before/after logs and dirty score
  • Offline-capable frontend + self-hosted backend
  • Extensible: easy to add custom rules, LLM integration, or advanced imputation later

CSV Cleaning Bot is currently in active development (V0.4). The goal is to make data cleaning fast, safe and enjoyable for everyone.

Feedback, feature requests, or contributions are always welcome!
