Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?

CSV (.csv) and Excel (.xls, .xlsx). Multiple files can be uploaded at once.

How is the dirty score calculated?

Percentage of cells that are missing (including blanks) or duplicates (lower is better).

Is my data safe?

Yes — everything is processed in memory. No data is stored or sent anywhere.

What cleaning happens automatically?

Missing values filled, text normalized (lowercase + strip), duplicates & outliers removed.
